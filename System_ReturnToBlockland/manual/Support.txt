<font:Palatino Linotype:24>Support<font:Palatino Linotype:18>
You can get support for problems you encounter with RTB from a number of places:

<font:Palatino Linotype Bold:20>RTB Forums<font:Palatino Linotype:18>
The forums are located at <a:www.returntoblockland.com/forums>http://www.returntoblockland.com/forums</a> where you can register and make a thread in the Helpdesk forum.

<font:Palatino Linotype Bold:20>Email<font:Palatino Linotype:18>
You can email support@returntoblockland.com regarding any problems or questions you have.

<font:Palatino Linotype Bold:20>Bug Reporting<font:Palatino Linotype:18>
You can report bugs by clicking the Report Bug button on the Mod Manager screen which will be picked up by RTB Developers.
